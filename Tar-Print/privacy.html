Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2020

Tar Print (“we” or “us” or “our”) respects the privacy of our users (“user” or “you”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Tar Print. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT ACCESS THE APP.

Tar Print does not collect or store any data in any form. We do not have any facilities to store any data from the user's device, and all processes are performed on the user's device itself. Tar Print is not affiliated with any third parties.

Users can allow or deny Tar Print access to their current location while using the app to get the coordinates and analyze surface types at their current location. Users can also allow or deny Tar Print access to make additions to their photo library if they would like to save analyzed images. With both features, no information is passed up to Tar Print nor saved anywhere.

We may change this policy from time to time and if we do, this page will be updated. If you continue using Tar Print after those changes are in effect, you agree to the new policy.
